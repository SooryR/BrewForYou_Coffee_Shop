How to make our Black Coffee

  1. Each coffee bean is checked for any deformities then is roasted to perfection and ground up to a mix of fine-grained and rough to get the strongest coffee flavour without the bitterness.
  2. Coldwater is filtered as the tap water has other minerals and chemicals that affect the flavour.
  3. Measured 2 tablespoons of our ground coffee for every 6 ounces or 3/4 cup of water.
  4. Water temperature is heated to between 90 degrees – 95 degrees celsius. We don't boil the water because it can cause the coffee ground to extract too much too early, leaving a bitter taste in your cup.
  5. Rinse filter paper with hot water before brewing. Use a good quality oxygen bleached filter.
  6. Pour just enough water over the grounds to get them wet and not soaked, to let them bloom.
  7. After 30-45 seconds continue the pour-over and wait for 5 mins. Then serve.
